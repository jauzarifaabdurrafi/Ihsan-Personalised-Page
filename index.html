A Note to Ihsan

Since 2023

I guess to me, you’re a bit like Doctor Strange, Ihsan. And I didn’t come to that randomly. Being your friend has always felt like having a walking paradox in my life. You can be blunt, almost too honest most of the time, but when it really matters, you’re deeply protective. You’d call me out on my bad decisions without holding back, then somehow still find a way to help fix things behind the scenes. I want you to know that when things fall apart, you’re the friend I’d want on my side. During my breakup, you felt like a brother figure to me, like someone who could already see how everything was going to end. You gave me advice that was blunt, sometimes frustrating, and honestly not what I wanted to hear at the time. But deep down, I knew you were right. Thank you for keeping me from completely falling apart. And now, looking back, I realize I probably should’ve listened more. My bad.

Author: Jauza Rifa Abdurrafi
